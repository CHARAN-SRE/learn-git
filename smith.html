Smith was returned to a formal leadership position in November 2021, named vice-captain to Pat Cummins. Australia's maiden appearance in the World Test Championship final was tailor made for Smith as he posted a memorable first-innings 121 as the Aussies lifted the mace for the first time. In November 2023, Smith added a third World Cup trophy to his illustrious resume (to go with the 2015 ODI and 2021 T20 World Cups) when he and Australia triumphed over India in the ODI tournament of that year
